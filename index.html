Research

As I dive deep into research, I strive to understand project goals and needs, creating a visual moodboard and establishing the foundation for typography, layout, motion, and color. I always maintain a strong connection between functionality and user engagement.

Design

Once the research is done, I move on to the design phase. Here, I go around like a rocket on the design exploration, creating concepts, developing brand identity, ensuring visual consistency, and planning for scalability.

Creation

As the design gains momentum, I pivot into the creation phase, where the whirl of interactive feedback fuels the development of an engaging product. Here, I infuse motion and interactivity to give life to aesthetics, uniting a clear intent with a memorable user experience.

Creative Development

After the creation phase, it's time for the handoff. Experience the magic of this stage as I add the final touches to the project, meticulously creating essential documentation. I maintain close contact with creative developers to bring the idealized interaction and experience to life with precision and dedication, just like pulling a rabbit out of a hat, turning the ordinary into the extraordinary.
